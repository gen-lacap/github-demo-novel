=============
CHARACTERS
=============
Jay Catsby - a young, mysterious millionaire with shady business connections.

Nick Carraway - Our narrator and guide throughout the story. He's a Yale University 
graduate from the Midwest.

Daisy Buchanan - a beautiful woman from Louisville, Kentucky, who was once in love with Catsby.

Thomas "Tom" Buchanan - a millionaire who lives in East Egg and Daisy's husband.

Myrtle Wilson
Tom's lover, whose lifeless husband George owns a run-down garage in the valley of ashes. Myrtle herself possesses a fierce vitality and desperately looks for a way to improve her situation.
